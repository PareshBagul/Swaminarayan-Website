Nadiad is a large city in the Kheda District of Gujarat. Nadiad is home to some of the largest academic institutions in the state, such as Dharmasinh Desai University, M. U. Patel Urological Research Institute, Mission Heart Institute, Maha Gujarat Hospital and Heart Center, and students from all over come to study in Nadiad. The BAPS Swaminarayan Chhatralaya was inaugurated in 1991 and is a modern hostel offering room and board at an affordable cost for college students. The hostel also provides delicious food, clean rooms, and a beautiful campus. Students are encouraged to follow basic moral disciplines. Sadhus oversee the students and provide basic academic and spiritual guidance. Students begin each morning with aarti and are encouraged to attend daily sabhas or assemblies in the evening. The BAPS Swaminarayan Chhatralaya, Nadiad provides the right mix of comfort, inspiration, and guidance for students to progress academically and spiritually.
