Mehsana is the largest city between Ahmedabad and the Rajasthani border. It is home to many of the state’s largest manufacturing plants and is also surrounded by lush farmlands. The BAPS Swaminarayan Chhatralaya, Mehsana was inaugurated in 1995. It is home to students primarily enrolled in science programs. The hostel is run by sadhus and volunteers that monitor each student’s academic and spiritual growth. Students are encouraged to participate in cultural programs and partake in service events in the local community. The hostel provides some of the finest facilities in the entire city. It is the perfect place for students to grow spiritually, academically, and morally.
