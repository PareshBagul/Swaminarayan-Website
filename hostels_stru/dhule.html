Dhule is in the northwestern part of Maharashtra and is a large city. Inaugurated in 1998, the BAPS Swaminarayan Chhatralaya in Dhule houses over a couple of hundred students. The hostel is affiliated with North Maharashtra University and provides special accommodations for students enrolled in that institution. The hostel provides comfortable accommodations for many students attending the fine academic institutions located in Dhule and Jalgoan. These students can seek moral and spiritual guidance from sadhus and can participate in daily prayer and rituals. Students are offered academic assistance. Students are encouraged to enroll in a variety of humanitarian initiatives run by the hostel and are given a chance to perform and participate in cultural programs in the local community. The hostel provides state-of-the-art lodging at an affordable price for students seeking to establish their academic and professional careers.
