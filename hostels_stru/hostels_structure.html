These educational hostels cater to a variety of demographics, including the underserved and tribal regions of western India. Ukai is one such example of an educational hostel that provides opportunities for young students from a tribal and economically disadvantaged background who would otherwise face extreme hardship in their studies. Ukai and the educational hostels operated by the BAPS Swaminarayan Sanstha stand as a testament to the power of education and service.
