Baroda is a major metropolis in Gujarat that attracts student from all over India. These students find the BAPS Swaminarayan Chhatralaya, Atladra to be a safe, clean, and healthy environment in which to live and learn. The all-boys hostel was established in 1983. It is only 5 kilometers away from the heart of the city. It is equipped with a modern library and computer lab. Free tuition and academic assistance programs help student tackle difficult coursework with ease. The facility is environmentally friendly as it uses solar power to heat water. Students are encouraged to attend daily spiritual assemblies and weekly cultural programs. Weekly youth forums make sadhus and volunteers easily accessible for students to approach with concerns or issues. Sports and recreation facilities on the campus provide students with an opportunity to participate in extracurricular activities. The hostel provides the perfect environment for students to grow spiritually, culturally, and academically.
